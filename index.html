Aims

The mission of our company is to alleviate the problems people experience when seeking and approaching things which connected to their daily lives. The philosophy of our company is twofold: help more people increases the chance of meeting the right things they need; and a pre-selection mechanism is needed to save time and resources.

In the long run, it is hoped that our products will have a positive impact on society and make people lives into a more vibrant one.

Our Team

Michael SU

Michael holds a Degree in Biology from Imperial College London, a Masters in Educational Research from Oxford University and a conversion degree in Psychology from the University of Hong Kong.


He worked as a research assistant at the Assessment Research Centre of the Hong Kong Institute of Education where he conducted psychology and psychometric research using quantitative research methods.


Michael’s research interests are in creativity, academic attainment and social psychology. His research experiences have motivated him to popularize the use of psychometric tools to solve real-life problems. Michael’s role in the current project includes formulating the questionnaire and the matching algorithm, wire-framing the functions and designing the user interface of the app, and managing the marketing and promotional aspects of the project. Michael completed a short course in Graphic Design at Central Saint Martins and is currently enrolled in a User Experience Design course at General Assembly.

Rex CHIM

Rex holds a Degree in Computer Science and a Masters in Social Science from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.


During his employment at the Assessment Research Centre, he was responsible for conducting statistical modelling, developing an online application system for the centre, and optimizing a widely-used educational computer programme called SP Xpress.


Rex aims to leverage his programming knowledge to make the current project a reality. His role in the project involves the development, testing, implementation, enhancement and maintenance of the mobile app and web platform (both front and back ends). He is also responsible for managing the accounting and financial aspects of the project.
